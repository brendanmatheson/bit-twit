Want to be Happy, Rich, Powerful? Or all of the above? Don't we all. That's why we work, right? Why we go to college. Why we go to school. But what happens when you're in a job you don't love? You've finished university and school and now those doors are shut and the next generation is being sheperded through. You're on the conveyor belt and it's direction is one way. Did you make the right decisions?

This service will guide you to a better career and a better life. Are you having a career change of heart, a career stall, a career crisis? LifeMap takes the strategies of your role models and peers and transforms their stories into a step-by-step course of action you can apply to your own life.
